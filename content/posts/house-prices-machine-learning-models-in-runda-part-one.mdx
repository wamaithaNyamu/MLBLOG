Runda is a suburb in the heart of Nairobi. Its an estate of the who is who in the country. Due to the status quo of
the class of citizens that live here, it is expected that the price of land and houses are off the roof (well, from the common mwananchis perspective anyway). This notebook walks through investigating housing prices in Runda. The data has been scrapped from BuyRentKenya. Full code for this tutorial series can be found [here](https://github.com/wamaithaNyamu/RUNDAHOUSES/) .
